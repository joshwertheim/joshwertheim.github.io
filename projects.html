SpotifyNotifier

Handy, small, efficient.

The SpotifyNotifier[SN] lives in your OSX Dock while you listen to music on Spotify. Its purpose is to tell you what is currently playing. SN will display a native OSX notification with four track-related details:

  • Title
  • Album
  • Artist
  • Cover Art

The first three are very useful to know, especially while shuffling through large playlists that you aren't very familiar with. The cover art image is very small, but adds just a touch of personality to each notification banner.

This app supports two helpful features. It has a default global hotkey, fn-back-quote, which will re-display the notification - useful if you miss it the first time. It can also hide the dock icon.

SFGinfo

Source for SF Giants web application hosted on Google App Engine.

Currently built using Google's webapp2 framework and Python. Code is loosely based on my earlier sandlotbot project.

Still very much a work in progress. I'm planning to make it look a lot nicer. It'll also be much more functional than just a simple news reader.

Upon opening, its root index is simple with just two links. One goes to the news page and the other goes to the roster page.
